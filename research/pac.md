# Plasma-Assisted Combustion (PAC)

Plasma-assisted combustion using Nanosecond Repetitively Pulsed Discharges (NRPDs) is an emerging technology that enhances the reactivity of fuel-air mixtures, offering significant improvements in operational and fuel flexibility — two crucial features for future sustainable gas turbines. My work on PAC spans three main subtopics:

- [Thermoacoustic stabilization](/research/pac/thermoacoustic-stabilization/)
- [Combustion enhancement](/research/pac/combustion-enhancement/)
- [Nitrogen oxides chemistry](/research/pac/nox-chemistry/)
